Problem

Nearly 1 in 4 women in their menstruating years experience “period poverty,” ranging from the inability to purchase the products they need to the inability to go to work, school, and get out in life in general because of it.

Solution

We offer high quality products to our members including PMS care, menstraul care and birth control. Paid memberships contribute to C3 free dispensearies that will be placed in low-income schools, homeless shelters and jails.
